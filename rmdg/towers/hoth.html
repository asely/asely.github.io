Tower History

1552 Record of three bells.
1607 Three bells recast by Joseph Hatch.
1741 Treble cast by Thomas Lester to make ring of four.
1758 Second cracked.
1762 Front three recast by Lester & Pack, treble also cast to make five.
1927 Tenor recast, treble added and bells rehung by Alfred Bowell to make present ring of six.
c.1930
Hothfield Ringers - the Woolley Family shortly after the rehanging and augmentation of the bells to 6

Front row:  ERNEST    EDWIN    ALBERT
1961 Bells restored by ringers from surrounding areas.
1997 Restoration work undertaken by the ASELY / RM&DG.
2007 Bells cleaned and bird nesting & associated litter removed from the bellchamber following reshingling of the spire.
