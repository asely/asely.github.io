Tower History

1440's Current Clock bell cast by William Chamberlain.
1600's Sanctus bell cast.
1620 Tenor bell cast by Joseph Hatch.
1685 Treble, second, fourth and fifth added to William Chamberlain and Joseph Hatch bells make ring of six.
1900 Bells recast to eight and hung in a cast iron frame.  William Chamberlain bell hung as a clock bell.
1969 All bells rehung and staple drilled out of clock bell.  Bells rededicated on 14th September.
2007 Frame strengethened and tied and bell fittings checked an nuts tightened etc, marginally improving the 'go' of the bells

Ringing Times

Practice: Friday 7:30pm
Sunday: Weekly 10:00am

N.B.  Bells rung mainly by a local band; some of whom are affiliated to the Guild as outlined below.  The Guild usually attends the practice once monthly on the 3rd Friday to assist.
